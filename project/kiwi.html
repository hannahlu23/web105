Kiwis have a sweet but tart taste to them. The outer skin of a kiwi is soft, brown and fuzzy. Most people will peel the skin or eat around it but the skin is edible, it just has a funny texture as it is fuzzy.

Some of kiwis many benefits are, helping treat asthma, aids digestion, boosts the immune system, can help manage blood pressure and reduces blood clotting.

Kiwi is native to China, in 2018 China produced half of the world total of kiwi. Kiwi can grow best in China, New Zealand and Italy.

Kiwi got its name because of a bird named Kiwi, someone stated that the fruit looked like the birds fur.
