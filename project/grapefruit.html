Grapefruit

Grapefruit is a hit or miss fruit for most people, I believe you either love it or hate it. It has a very sour tart taste to it but if you get the perfect one, its so sweet and delicious. Many people will sprinkle some sugar over their grapefruit before they eat it to mellow out the tartness.

Grapefruits are known as one of the healthiest fruits, it can benefit your immune system, promote appetite control, aid in weight loss, help prevent insulin resistance and diabetes, can improve heart health, high in antioxidants, and can reduce the risk of kidney stones.

Grapefruit originate in Barbados, it is an accidental cross between an orange and a pomelo. You can find grapefruit growing in Florida, California, Texas, South America, Australia, and South Africa.
