Besides the fact that blueberries taste so delicious, they are a fruit that has so many health benefits!

Blueberries are well known for being an antioxidant, it is one of the highest levels of all fruits and vegetables. Blueberries protect cholesterol in your blood from becoming damaged, they can lower blood pressure, can help prevent heart disease, can help maintain brain function and improve memory and can help reduce muscle damage.

I personally got to go blueberry picking this summer, twice. I went to a little farm in Michigan, the first time I went was in July and they blueberries were so large, and so sweet and tasty. Then I went again in August and the difference in size and taste was drastic! The blueberries we so much tinier and tarter, was a little disappointed because I thought they were going to be that large size again but apparently blueberries are larger in the earlier season.
